Crypto Tax by Country

How much crypto tax do I pay in India? 🇮🇳

India taxes all crypto gains at a flat 30% + 4% Health and Education Cess = 31.2% effective rate. No deductions allowed, no loss offsets. Applies to all VDAs (Virtual Digital Assets) since April 2022.

Is crypto tax-free in Germany? 🇩🇪

Yes — crypto held more than 1 year is completely tax-free in Germany (§23 EStG). Under 1 year: 25% Abgeltungssteuer + 5.5% solidarity surcharge. One of Europe's most crypto-friendly tax regimes.

How is crypto taxed in the UK? 🇬🇧

UK CGT: 10% for basic rate taxpayers, 20% for higher rate (income over £50,270). Annual exempt amount of £3,000. HMRC treats crypto as property, not currency.

Is crypto tax-free in Singapore or UAE? 🇸🇬🇦🇪

Yes to both. Singapore has no capital gains tax. UAE has no personal income tax. Crypto gains are 0% in both countries for individual investors.

How is crypto taxed in the US? 🇺🇸

Short-term (under 1 year): ordinary income rates 10–37%. Long-term (over 1 year): 0%, 15%, or 20% depending on income. Plus state taxes. The IRS treats crypto as property.
